01 What we build

Software you can verify, not just believe in.

Three kinds of work we tend to do well. Each one starts with a narrow problem that matters and ends with something a team can actually point to.

  • Compliance & ops tools

    Registers, trackers, workflows. Things that have to be right, traceable, and still fast to use on a Tuesday afternoon.

  • Bespoke internal software

    Replacing spreadsheets that have outgrown themselves. Small products, built with the people who'll use them every day.

  • AI-enabled workflows

    AI used as an enabler inside reliable processes — with the receipts. Prompts, sources, and decisions you can show a regulator or a sceptical colleague.

02 How we work

Four steps. No surprises in the middle.

Our approach is intentionally compact. We begin with the operational reality, then build the smallest useful system that improves outcomes and can be maintained by the team using it.

  1. 01 · Understand

    Map the actual process

    Decisions, handoffs, constraints, and failure points. Conversations and artefacts before any contract.

  2. 02 · Define

    Scope a thin slice

    A lean product or bespoke build with clear boundaries — discovered by trying the wrong version first.

  3. 03 · Deliver

    Ship, then thicken

    Practical increments validated with real users. Deploy early. Keep the surface small.

  4. 04 · Stabilize

    Docs, tests, and keys

    Traceability, sensible controls, and a team that can run the product without us in the loop.
