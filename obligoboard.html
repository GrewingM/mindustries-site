Pre-built obligation frameworks

Expert-designed templates for GDPR, ESG, and CSRD/ESRS. Each obligation comes with plain-language guidance, legal references, and evidence requirements. Pick a framework and start tracking in minutes.

Evidence collection

Upload documents, screenshots, and reports directly against each obligation. Build a timestamped evidence trail that proves exactly when and how you met each requirement.

Audit-ready evidence packs

Generate structured PDF reports with obligations, statuses, owners, and evidence in one click. Hand them to your auditor, legal team, or board with confidence.

Dashboard & deadline tracking

Colour-coded statuses, risk levels, and due dates at a glance. Email reminders ensure nothing slips through the cracks. Assign owners and track accountability across your team.
